Superbuilder

Analytics Tools to improve Field Operations for Indian Construction Companies

Superbuilder Helps Your Construction Company Improve Productivity in the Field, Avoid Delays and Stay Within Budget

At the root of these problems is Inefficiencies in Field Operations. Superbuilder builds software to objectively measure every aspect of Field Operations. Powered by Superbuilder, construction companies get a data-driven perspective of progress on the field.
The SuperScore along with personalized insights make for strong incentives for field workers to objectively assess their performance and be more productive.

How it Works

Step 1: Collect Data

Feed construction schedules, tasks, material records into the Superbuilder platform.

Step 2: Organize Data

Superbuilder generates data visualizations to summarize information at a Macro level - Project Progress, Optimal Construction Schedules and Utilization Rates for Labor and Material

Step 3: Measurements and Insights

Measure individual worker’s performance with the ‘SuperScore’. Personalized insights that help individuals review their performance on the field and make improvements
