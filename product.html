Take Charge of Your Construction Project

Managing a construction project comprises several challenging manual tasks: preparing a schedule, assessing labor, material and tools required for a job, routing material requests, reviewing progress and revising schedules exhausts the human construction manager. Unlike Excel spreadsheets or traditional project management tools, Superbuilder enables you to take charge of your construction projects like never before and achieve operational excellence.

How SuperBuilder Works Now

SuperBuilder is a cloud based application that consists of three key functionalities to make life easy for the Indian construction worker:

  • Scheduling: this section is the control center for users in charge of planning and scheduling tasks. A simplified way to create, track and manage construction schedules
  • Materials Management: submit requests for materials, update and track status for material orders. Track spending and utilization of materials
  • Track tasks on-site: assign, delegate, review and track progress of every single construction task happening on the field

How SuperBuilder Will Evolve

What sets SuperBuilder apart from traditional construction management applications is that we have built a 'Foundational Construction Data Layer' that contains an extensive digital log of activity happening on the field. We plan to leverage this to launch the following features:

  • Integrations with existing ERP tools to let data flow seamlessly into and out of SuperBuilder
  • AI powered algorithms to optimize Scheduling and Planning
  • Algorithms to predict (and warn against) delays and budget overruns
  • Actionable insights for individual users to help them build up on-the-job skills
  • Game theory and mechanism design to enhance collaboration between team members
